Bio: I did my PhD at Inria Saclay in the DataShape team, under the supervision of Steve Oudot. Here is a link to my manuscript and here is one to my CV. I am currently holding a postdoctoral position in the Rabadan Lab at Columbia University.

Research interest: my research focuses on topological data analysis (TDA) and statistical machine learning (ML). I contributed to the analysis of topological descriptors and their use in ML methods such as kernel-SVM or deep learning.

Coding: my favorite languages are C++ and Python, but I also know a bit of R, Matlab and Java. I am also very familiar with scikit-learn and tensorflow. Here is a list of coding projects I have been involved in:

  • Cover complex package in the Gudhi C++ library ,
  • NIPS article code in the NIPS 2017 Implementation Challenge,
  • sklearn_tda: a scikit-learn compatible python package for TDA.
